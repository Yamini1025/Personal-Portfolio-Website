Projects

HTML-to-PDF Script

Worked with a team of 5 to build a scalable Python-based document generation pipeline to automatically produce PDFs from HTML, including cover pages, tables of contents, bookmarks, and intact internal links. The result improved reliability and efficiency by testing performance and resolving memory and navigation issues, streamlining technical publishing workflows.

Instance Segmentation for Autonomous Driving

Contributed to a team project to develop and evaluate a production-ready instance segmentation pipeline using Mask R-CNN, trained on 100K+ images from BDD100K and Cityscapes, with strong generalization across varied driving scenarios. We performed in-depth evaluation and visualization of segmentation performance, comparing Mask R-CNN with foundation-model approaches (SAM with text-conditioned detectors) to assess accuracy, trade-offs, and deployment suitability for autonomous driving.
